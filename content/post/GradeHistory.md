# Grade History Spreadsheet

This spreadsheet makes it very easy to calculate your CGPA.

[Grade History Spreadsheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/19a68PIKOImr63zxaMRBh5_TT5QhfCflCdVP_iLUBAFA/edit?usp=sharing)

## Usage
1. Open the Google Sheet Link
2. File > Make a copy
3. Rename your document > Make a copy
4. Now you can edit your own sheet

## Features
Dynamic spreadsheet, which calculates your CGPA based on the data you provide.

There are 2 sheets: Grades and Results

Grades: This is where you have to put your grade data

Results: This is where you get all the Results with distribution of your grades
